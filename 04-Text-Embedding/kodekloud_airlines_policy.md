## KodeKloud Airlines — Airline Policy Document (KKA-POL-001)

### 1) About KodeKloud Airlines
- **Airline name**: KodeKloud Airlines (KKA)
- **Primary hub**: Bengaluru Kempegowda International Airport (**BLR**)
- **Customer support hours**: **06:00–22:00 IST** (daily)
- **Support email**: **support@kodekloudairlines.com**
- **Support phone**: **+91-80-12345678**

### 2) Fleet and cabins
- **Fleet (training demo)**: **Boeing 737-800**, **Airbus A320neo**
- **Cabins**: Economy, Business

### 3) Baggage policy
#### 3.1 Cabin baggage (carry-on)
- **1 cabin bag** per passenger, max **7 kg**
- **Max dimensions**: **55 × 40 × 23 cm**
- **1 personal item** allowed (laptop bag/handbag) that fits under the seat

#### 3.2 Checked baggage (included allowance)
- **Economy**: **1 piece up to 23 kg**
- **Business**: **2 pieces up to 32 kg each**

#### 3.3 Excess baggage
- If your checked baggage exceeds the included allowance, the fee is **₹500 per kg** (rounded up to the next whole kg).

### 4) Ticket changes and cancellations
#### 4.1 Cancellations by the passenger (refund)
- **48+ hours before departure**: **100% refund** of base fare
- **24–48 hours before departure**: **50% refund** of base fare
- **Less than 24 hours before departure**: **No refund**

#### 4.2 Date/time changes (rebooking)
- Rebooking is allowed up to **2 hours** before departure.
- A **change fee of ₹1,000** per passenger applies, plus any fare difference.

### 5) Check-in and boarding
- **Online check-in opens**: **48 hours** before departure
- **Online check-in closes**: **60 minutes** before departure
- **Boarding gate closes**: **20 minutes** before departure

### 6) Unaccompanied minors (UM)
- Allowed age: **5–12 years**
- Mandatory UM service fee: **₹2,500 per sector**
- A guardian must complete the UM form at the departure airport.

### 7) Pets in cabin (training demo policy)
- Allowed pets: **small dogs and cats only**
- Must travel in an approved carrier that fits under the seat
- **Max combined weight (pet + carrier)**: **8 kg**
- Fee: **₹4,000 per sector**
- Pets are **not permitted** in Business cabin on this training policy.

### 8) Loyalty program — KodeKloud SkyMiles
- Earn rate: **1 mile per ₹50** spent on base fare
- Tier thresholds:
  - **Silver**: **10,000 miles**
  - **Gold**: **25,000 miles**
- Tier benefits (summary):
  - Silver: priority check-in (Economy)
  - Gold: priority check-in + extra 5 kg checked baggage allowance (Economy)

### 9) Travel documents (domestic travel)
- Passengers must carry a valid government-issued photo ID for check-in.

### 10) Notes for this course
This policy document is intentionally written with clear, specific facts (numbers, thresholds, fees, contacts) so learners can test semantic search with a vector database.
